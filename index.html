Hemp Calculator

Innovative tool for calculating optimal consumption of hemp products based on your individual parameters and goals. It helps determine the ideal amount of protein, omega fatty acids, and other nutrients you can get from hemp products.

Calculator Features

  • Calculation of optimal amount of protein from hemp
  • Determination of omega-3 and omega-6 fatty acid balance
  • Recommendations for hemp product consumption to achieve your goals
  • Consideration of individual characteristics and dietary preferences

Benefits of Hemp Nutrition

Hemp products are rich in complete protein, contain an ideal ratio of omega fatty acids, and have anti-inflammatory properties. Regular consumption of hemp can contribute to improved heart health, skin condition, and overall well-being.
